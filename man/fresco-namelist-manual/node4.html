Input NAMELIST format

The namelist input has two fixed-format lines to start with, and these followed by the namelists in Fortran style free-format. Variable names may be upper or lower case without distinction, but in this manual they are all capitalized. All namelists are terminated by a /. Some systems trailing comments begun by a !.
