Test run: a CCBA calculation

The following test case calculates the inelastic and transfer cross sections for the reactions induced by F on Si at 60.0 MeV. A Triton-Oxygen cluster structure is assumed for F, with the potentials for the triton and the oxygen core being folded together to give a diagonal Watanabe potential for elastic F scattering as well as the couplings to the inelastic 1/2+ state. These elastic and inelastic channels are coupled together to all orders, then a finite-range transfer step is calculated for the O + P channel, thus performing a CCBA calculation.
